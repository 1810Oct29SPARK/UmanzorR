Cryptid Locations

Cryptid sightings have been reported all over the world. These are the locations where

each cryptid is most commonly seen.

  • Sasquatch- Seen all over North America
  • Mothman- Most commonly seen in North Viginia
  • Champ- Located in Lake Champlain
  • Wendigo- Located in South Canada
  • Loveland Frog- Located in Loveland, OH
  • Melon Heads- Located in Michigan and Ohio.
  • The Goatman- Located in Maryland
  • The Jersey Devil- Located in New Jersey.
  • Jackalope- Located largely in North America.
  • Honey Island Swamp Monster- Located in Louisiana
