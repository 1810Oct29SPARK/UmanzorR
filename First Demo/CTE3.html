Litagation on Behalf of CTE victims


Lawsuit

Since the beginning of the 2010 decade, former NFL players filed a class action Lawsuit against the NFL in order to recieve

compensation after experiencig symptoms of CTE. They argue that the NFL has been neglient in terms of supplying players with

information regarding the dangers of CTE and that the NFL has continuosly denied that there is a connection between playing football

and the onset of CTE.


The Settlement

In early 2017 the NFL reached a settlment with the players and agreed to pay over a billion dollars to over 20,000 former NFL players.
